The freshest ingredients...

Here at Francesco's, we only use the freshest ingredients. We have agreements with several local suppliers to bring you the highest quality foods. Our vegetables are mostly locally sourced, and in-season. Our fresh meats come from sustainable local ranchers.

We also take great care in preparing our meals. All our pizza doughs are hand-tossed using authentic, traditional methods.

Who is Francesco...

In 1934, at the age of 22, Francesco Caruso left his home in Sicily to start a new life in America. He arrived in New York only a few dollars, and the address of a distant cousin. That cousin gave him his first restaurant job as a dishwasher, and later his second job as a cook.

In 1950, Francesco took his years of experience to the west coast, where he opened a restaurant in San Jose (the first location was on 1st street). Francesco passed away in 1988, but his legacy lives on.
